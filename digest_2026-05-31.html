The Bangalore Metro Rail Corporation Limited (BMRCL) has submitted a Detailed Project Report for a nearly 60-km metro corridor from Madavara (BIEC) to Tumakuru via Nelamangala, Dobbspet, and Kyathasandra, estimated at up to Rs 19,361 crore under a Viability Gap Funding-based PPP model.
