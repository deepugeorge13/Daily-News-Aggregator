Bengaluru Startup GalaxEye Launches India's Largest Private Satellite, World's First OptoSAR, aboard SpaceX Falcon 9
Bengaluru-based spacetech startup GalaxEye successfully launched Mission Drishti on May 3 — the world's first OptoSAR satellite and India's largest privately built Earth observation satellite, weighing 190 kg — aboard a SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket from Vandenberg, California. The satellite integrates optical and Synthetic Aperture Radar sensors into a single platform for all-weather, day-and-night imaging, and GalaxEye plans to scale it into a 10-satellite constellation by 2030.
