India and the United Kingdom have officially confirmed July 15, 2026 as the entry-into-force date for the Comprehensive Economic and Trade Agreement (CETA), which will grant duty-free access to 99% of India's tariff lines in the UK market across sectors including textiles, marine products, leather, and engineering goods.
